James Heald

I am a Research Fellow In Theoretical Neuroscience working with Maneesh Sahani at the Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit, UCL.

I received a PhD in Engineering from the University of Cambridge in 2020. I was trained in the Computational and Biological Learning Lab under the supervision of Daniel Wolpert (primary supervisor, now at Columbia) and Máté Lengyel* (secondary advisor). Prior to my PhD, I worked as a medical doctor in the NHS. Before that, I studied an MSc in Cognitive and Computational Neuroscience at The University of Sheffield and an MBChB in Medicine at The University of Manchester.
